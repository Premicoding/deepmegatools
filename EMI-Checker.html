What is EMI?

EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment. It is the fixed amount a borrower pays every month to repay a loan within a specific period.

The EMI amount includes:

  • Principal Loan Amount
  • Interest Amount
  • Loan Tenure

EMI Calculation Formula

The standard EMI calculation formula is:

EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N – 1]
Symbol Meaning
P Loan Amount
R Monthly Interest Rate
N Loan Tenure in Months
