What Is Aspect Ratio?

Aspect Ratio is the proportional relationship between an image's width and height. Maintaining the correct aspect ratio ensures that your image looks natural and does not appear stretched or distorted after resizing.

For most resizing tasks, keeping the aspect ratio locked is highly recommended. This preserves the original proportions while adjusting the overall dimensions.

Best Practices for Image Optimization
